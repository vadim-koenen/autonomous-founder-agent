## Initial Portfolio

### Cash: Agent Launch QA Sprint

- Thesis: teams shipping agents will pay for a concrete launch gate because live buyers request evals, schema validation, kill switches, and observability.
- Buyer: indie AI founders and small teams with a working agent, MCP server, or LLM workflow approaching launch.
- Offer: 25 launch cases, prioritized defect report, one agreed repair patch, and rerun within 48 hours.
- Price: $149 fixed.
- Discovery: public GitHub proof and intake first; Contra or Upwork only after connection.
- Payment: Contra guest checkout after qualified purchase intent and owner verification; marketplace escrow for a marketplace-originated buyer.
- 72-hour proof: 25 public target records, three public-safe audit hooks, and one qualified interest signal after distribution begins.
- Kill: 25 qualified contacts and zero replies.

### Asset: Agent Launch Gate

- Thesis: a reusable launch decision is more valuable than generic information when it contains runnable cases and a report contract.
- Buyer: indie teams that want repeatable testing without a full service.
- Offer: 25 cases, scoring rules, report schema, regression checklist, and adaptation notes.
- Price: $39 founding license.
- Discovery: free GitHub sample and public interest intake.
- Payment: Contra digital-product checkout only after three qualified interest signals and owner verification.
- 72-hour proof: publish the five-case sample, report schema, and collect at least one qualified request.
- Kill: 100 qualified sample views and zero qualified interest signals.

### Frontier: Agent Opportunity Pulse

- Thesis: agent builders may pay for normalized comparisons and caveats that save work beyond the free raw Bazaar feed.
- Buyer: x402 service operators and agent builders evaluating what to build or integrate.
- Offer: machine-readable category ranking using public demand, payer-breadth, listing-quality, and competition signals.
- Price: 0.25 USDC per snapshot.
- Discovery: free GitHub sample and 25 public x402 operator targets.
- Payment: x402 only after three endpoint requests, a recurring use case, and an owner-configured dedicated wallet.
- 72-hour proof: publish the normalized sample and gather explicit endpoint or integration requests.
- Kill: 250 qualified sample fetches and zero endpoint requests.
