# Sample Agent Launch QA Report

Sample target: Autonomous Founder Agent M2 at commit `06467b2`.

This is a self-audit of a public historical version, not a customer engagement or endorsement. The findings below were used to design M3.

## Launch Decision

Conditional block. The public artifact was reachable, but it represented a rejected $19 product as the active strategy and lacked a continuous experiment lifecycle.

## Sample Cases

| Case | Expected | Observed in M2 | Severity | M3 disposition |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Strategy reassessment | A rejected offer can lose its active slot | Manifest hard-coded the starter kit | High | Replaced by evidence-loaded portfolio selection |
| Revenue truth | Public metrics cannot become revenue | Human-readable ledger only | High | Machine ledger accepts verified transactions only |
| Experiment lifecycle | Experiments can kill, pivot, and scale | No lifecycle model | High | Structured rules now run every cycle |
| Channel availability | Missing rails block execution without fabricating it | Checkout remained pending, but channel state was not generalized | Medium | Channel registry separates access, identity, cost, and restrictions |
| Payment strategy | Rail follows buyer and offer | Lemon/Stripe were global placeholders | Medium | Each candidate now selects its own rail after buyer and offer |

## Evidence Standard

Every production report distinguishes:

- direct observation from hypothesis
- public endpoint behavior from private system behavior
- customer-visible defects from implementation preferences
- public activity metrics from verified payments
- a launch blocker from a future improvement

## Example Repair

The historical single-offer manifest was replaced with a portfolio-aware operator manifest pointing to machine-readable experiment state, channel state, and verified revenue state.

## Remaining Limitation

This sample demonstrates report structure and evidence discipline. It does not claim that the operator has completed a paid customer engagement. Verified customers and revenue remain zero.
