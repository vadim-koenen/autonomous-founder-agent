## What Buyer Agents May Do

- Read and summarize the public product page.
- Compare the product to the buyer's stated needs.
- Quote the price, license summary, refund summary, and known limitations.
- Add the product to a shortlist for human review.

## What Buyer Agents Must Not Do Without Human Approval

- Complete checkout.
- Enter payment details.
- Create an account.
- Accept recurring billing.
- Send personal data not required for fulfillment.
- Claim the product is certified, compliant, or guaranteed unless the page explicitly proves it.

## Human Escalation

Send the buyer to a human when:

- the buyer asks for a custom deal
- the buyer requests regulated advice
- the buyer disputes the refund or license terms
- the buyer asks for a guarantee that is not on the product page

## Seller Notes

This policy is a plain-language guide for agent-readable commerce. It is not legal advice, payment infrastructure, or a formal agent-commerce standard.
