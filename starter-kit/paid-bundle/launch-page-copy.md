# Agent-to-Agent Commerce Starter Kit

Make your digital product easier for buyer agents to understand.

Autonomous buyer agents are starting to inspect products, compare offers, and ask humans for purchase approval. Most product pages are still written only for people. This kit helps you publish a simple machine-readable product card, agent purchase policy, and seller checklist before the ecosystem fully standardizes.

## What You Get

- Product-card JSON template
- Agent purchase policy template
- Seller QA checklist
- Completed example product card
- Free sample product card
- Storefront listing copy

## Who It Is For

- indie software makers
- digital download sellers
- prompt-pack creators
- template sellers
- paid report authors
- small tool builders

## What It Helps With

- Clarify price, deliverables, refund terms, license terms, and buyer fit.
- Tell agents when to ask for human approval.
- Make the product easier to compare against buyer intent.
- Reduce confusion before checkout.

## What It Is Not

- Not legal advice.
- Not payment infrastructure.
- Not an NFT drop.
- Not a guarantee of sales.
- Not a formal certification or compliance standard.
