What The Kit Packages

Product Metadata

Clear fields for buyer fit, deliverables, price, refund summary, license summary, and limitations.

Agent Policy

Plain-language instructions for what buyer agents may summarize, shortlist, and escalate before checkout.

Seller Checklist

A fast QA pass so the public page and machine-readable product card do not contradict each other.

Launch Boundary

  • No payment is enabled from this static page.
  • No NFT is minted from this project.
  • No email, ad, public post, wallet transaction, or trading action is performed by the code.
  • The paid bundle is prepared as a draft until an approved storefront is connected.

Selected by the M1 scoring engine because it is fast to package, low-cost, high-margin, timely, and mostly buildable by the agent itself.
